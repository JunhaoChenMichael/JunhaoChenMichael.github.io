Summary

A low frequency picture and a high frequency image are combined to create the hybrid image. The edges of an image's high frequency components are sharply varying pixel values, whereas the low frequency components are blurred color blocks with no obvious borders between them. When the picture is close to the observer, the edge details are clearly captured by the eyes, however when the image is far away, the color blocks of the image dominate. And the it is very interesting to hybrid features of two images into the final result.
